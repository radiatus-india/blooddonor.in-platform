# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and useful for people working on public-interest health coordination software.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent
- focus on helping contributors improve the code and documentation
- give actionable review feedback

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- discrimination
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
